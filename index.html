Our Story

Innovating Since 2017

We're a team of passionate tech professionals based in Auckland, New Zealand, dedicated to delivering cost-effective, high-quality IT solutions that drive real business value.

Kounga Solutions was founded with a simple mission: make advanced technology accessible and affordable for businesses of all sizes. Whether you're a fast-growing SME or an established enterprise, we bring the same level of expertise and commitment to every project.

Our team combines deep technical knowledge with real-world business experience, allowing us to deliver solutions that don't just work, they excel. From custom app development and test automation to web design and strategic IT consulting, we're your end-to-end tech partner.
